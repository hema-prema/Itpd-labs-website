What's here?

Welcome to the unofficial lab of the EECE (Electrical Engineering and Computer Engineering) department, where we foster an environment that nurtures the growth of students and transforms their ideas into reality. We are the IPDc (Innovation to Development Cell), a space dedicated to empowering innovators. Here, we believe that anyone with a vision and a desire to make a difference can join our community. Together, we embrace diversity, collaboration, and the relentless pursuit of groundbreaking solutions. Join us on this extraordinary journey, where your ideas will be celebrated and your potential unleashed. Together, we can shape a brighter future.
